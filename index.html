Typer Drive

Created by Leo Chung

Instructions
Typer Drive is a typing-based combat game designed to train typing accuracy and speed.

Press Start to start a game or click Restart to restart.

The enemy will periodically attack the player, dealing a scaling amount of damage; the scale depends on how many enemies you have defeated!

Once the enemy is defeated, the player will heal some health and continue fighting another enemy!

Type for as long as you can!
